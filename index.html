About

As of June 2025, I am working in the Theory of Atoms research group of Professor Ludmil Katzarkov at the Institute of Mathematics and Informatics at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, Sofia, Bulgaria. I received my PhD in May 2025 from Columbia University, where I worked on motivic homotopy theories of stacks and functor formalisms under the supervision of Andrew Blumberg and Aise Johan de Jong. I am generally interested in various topics from homotopy theory and algebraic geometry, including K-theory, derived algebraic geometry, equivariant and global homotopy theory, and TQFTs and factorization homology.
